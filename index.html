Make your filesystem soundful.

Koto is a music performing filesystem, or a filesystem (sound) synthesizer. Koto allows to perform music by interaction with mounted FUSE filesystem. If you don't make sense with it, check this demo movies below.

Features

  • Koto is a free Software
  • Koto runs on GNU/Linux and macOS
  • Koto has basic oscillators and sequencers
  • Koto has some effects and utilities
