Make your filesystem soundful.

Koto is a music performing filesystem, or a filesystem (sound) synthesizer.
Koto allows to perform music by interaction with mounted FUSE filesystem.

Sound & Music

Koto generates sound and music nicely.
We get nice groove with interacting Koto.

Filesystem

Koto uses filesystem as a UI.
You can check and change melodies or parameters of sound modules by reading, writing deleting and creating files/directories.
There is two way to interact the filesystem. First is using shell commands, second is using GUI filer. This makes you smile.

Modularity

Koto has a modular synthesizer architecture.
Oscillators, envelope generators and effects can be connected freely, like modular synthesizers.
